Our Story

We started our journey as ECDC in 2009. Now we rebranded to Aroom Interior Designs, recognized for combining an unmatched commitment to client service and experience in the field with professional expertise, Aroom has won the hearts for 15 years, helping clients to realize their dream projects and plans. With thousands of happy clients and smiling faces, Aroom is entering into another phase of its growth being renamed as Aroom Interiors.
