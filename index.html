The story behind the style

We began our journey as ECDC in 2012 and rebranded as Aroom Interior LLP in 2021. Known for our unwavering commitment to client service and professional expertise, Aroom has been transforming spaces and winning hearts for 13 years. With thousands of satisfied clients, Aroom Interiors is your trusted partner for Interior and Exterior Design, construction, Architectural planning, Modular kitchens, and Home styling solutions.

What We Offer

Residential Design: Transforming homes into timeless, elegant, and practical sanctuaries.
Commercial Design: Crafting inspiring and efficient environments for offices, retail spaces, and more.
Space Planning: Optimizing layouts to make the most of your space.
Furniture & Decor Selection: Curating pieces that complement the theme and vision of your space.
Renovation & Remodeling: Modernizing interiors with innovative solutions.

Design Philosophy

We prioritize collaboration, partnering closely with clients to grasp their needs and bring their visions to life. By blending innovative design trends with timless principles, we create projects that are both enduring and uniquely customized.
